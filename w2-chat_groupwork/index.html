Rules of Game

Everybody gets a word from the director. Four of you get a different word than others get. But they are similar. e.g.. Apple and Pineapple
Whoever gets the different ones, they do not know that they are the spy.
Go around the room and describe your word, in a sentence, another word, a metaphor, whatever you can come up with to type in and send to all.
You can not lie while describing your word, you can not mention the word itself.
After a round, everybody types in the name that they think is the spy to vote. Top of the vote gets kicked out by the director.
Which team survive in the end, wins. yay
